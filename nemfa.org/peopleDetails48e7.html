Biography:  Caleb Pillsbury is an experienced conductor and award winning music educator. He has taught Advanced Placement music theory and guitar and directed the many choirs at Mount Mansfield Union High School. For ten years Caleb has developed a choral program and music department regarded for its high amount of student participation and musical excellence. The choirs’ performances have been noted for their attention to nuance and beauty. Under his direction they have been invited twice to perform at Carnegie Hall in New York City, as well as exchange concerts and workshops in Philadelphia and Princeton, New Jersey. The choirs have also earned superior ratings at various choral festivals. Focusing on the development of musicianship and vocal development, many of Caleb’s students have participated in the New England Music Festival Chorus and National and Eastern Division Honor Choirs of the American Choral Director’s Association. Caleb holds an undergraduate degree from the University of Michigan and a Masters in Choral Conducting from the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama.
