Chorus Re-Hearing Schedule

All New England Chorus members will be heard performing a section or two of their music prior to the start of rehearsals on Thursday at Mt. Anthony.

Students will be heard in double quartets by NEMFA music educators. The complete policy is available in the Chorus Rehearing Policy document linked below.

Schools furthest away were schedule latest with those closest scheduled first. Please plan on arriving onsite in time for your choral students to get situated and perform their rehearing.

The schedule was emailed to all NEMFA Chorus teachers on Tuesday, March 4th. Contact Steffen Parker if you did not receive one.

CONCERT FESTIVAL DRESS

DRESS CODE for ALL THREE ENSEMBLES

Men

Dark suit (black preferred), dark shoes, dark socks, four-in-hand tie, and collared white shirt. No sport shoes or sandals. Black tuxedos (with the above accessories) are also acceptable.

Women

Black sleeved top (no bare midriff, shoulders, or low neckline) and a solid bottom (below-the-knees black skirt or black dress pants), black hose and dark shoes. No sport shoes or sandals. Long (below-the-knees) black gowns/dresses (with the above accessories) are also acceptable. All concert dress shall be in conservative good taste.
