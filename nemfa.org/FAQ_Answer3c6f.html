Frequently Asked Questions

What are the general responsibilities of a host family?

The requirements for host families are quite modest but important. The host families provide transportation to and from their home to the school, breakfast on Friday and Saturday and dinner on Friday, and sleeping accommodations for two nights (Thursday and Friday). A very few students who live far away may stay over an additional night.
