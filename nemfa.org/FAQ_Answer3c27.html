Frequently Asked Questions

What are the components of the New England Music Festival?

The New England Music Festival Association (NEMFA) Concert Festival is made up of the 500 most successful students heard in the performance auditions of the NEMFA Solo and Ensemble Festivals. From this group is formed a 250 voice chorus, a 125 piece orchestra, and a 125 piece symphonic band.
